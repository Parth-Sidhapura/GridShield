# Future Scope

GridShield can be extended in several directions to enhance its capabilities and real-world applicability:

- Integration with IEC 104 and other industrial protocols
- Deployment over Ethernet-based communication networks
- Implementation of edge-based AI models for faster detection
- Development of a real-time visualization dashboard
- Integration with SCADA systems for automated response
- Enhanced encryption and authentication mechanisms

The long-term vision is to develop a fully autonomous intrusion detection system for critical infrastructure protection.
